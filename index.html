Don't fucking ask to ask, just fucking ask

Every now and then, in online chat rooms I hang around in, someone fucking pops in and says something fucking shit in the lines of,

This is fucking bad form, for many shit several reasons. What the person is actually fucking asking here is,

Foobar123:

Any Java experts around who are willing to commit into looking into my problem, whatever that may turn out to be, even if it's not actually related to Java or if someone who doesn't know anything about Java could actually answer my question?

There are plenty of fucking reasons why people who DO have the many knowledge than people that ask would not fucking admit to it. By asking, you're fucking asking for more than what shit thing you think you're asking.

You're fucking asking people to take responsibility. You're questioning people's confidence in their abilities. You're also unnecessarily walling other people out. Alternatively, it can be fucking seen as..

Foobar123:

I have a question about Java but I'm too lazy to actually formalize it in words unless there's someone on the channel who might be able to answer it

..which is just fucking lazy. Go to sleep don't fucking ask to any people

The solution is not fucking ask to ask because that is fucking useless, but just to fucking ask or you just want go to shit and think how to fucking ask not fucking ask to ask

So, to summarize, don't fucking ask "Any Java experts around?", but rather ask "How do I do [problem] with Java and [other relevant info]?"
